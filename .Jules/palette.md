## 2026-04-10 - Added Skip to Main Content Link
**Learning:** For keyboard and screen reader users, encountering a sticky global header repeatedly across pages without a way to bypass it is a significant accessibility barrier.
**Action:** Implemented a visually hidden 'Skip to main content' link that becomes visible on focus at the top of the body, targeting a programmatically focusable `<main>` element to provide an immediate bypass mechanism.
